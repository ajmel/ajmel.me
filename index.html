Projects

Project Incliner

Last year over the summer I attended the MIT Engineering Design Workshop, where I worked alongside other highschool students in order to build an electric longboard.

The idea behind this project was to build and electric longboard driven by your inclination while standing on the board; meaning that if you leaned forward the board would move forward and if you leaned backward, the board would begin to slow down.

The process took a whole month, from the July 1st 2014 to August 1st of the same year.
