MIT EDGERTON CENTER

Established in 1992, the MIT Edgerton Center continues the hands-on legacy of Harold “Doc” Edgerton by giving students opportunities to learn by doing.

MIT EDGERTON CENTER ABOUT

I first discovered the MIT Edgerton Center through the Engineering Design Workshop. Since then I have been spending quite a lot of time here teaching and introducing younger students into the idea of engineering, art and science.

With the Edgerton Center I had the opportunity to travel to Beijing, China last March were I taught over 100 students about basic electronics, art and science. As I continue to spend time at the Edgerton Center I am only helping out maintain and clean-up the space.
